# Results Scaffold

## Corpus and coverage

Report the number of article–market pairs, source-type distribution, platform distribution, repeated article/market counts, market-quality coverage, and adjudication provenance. State the machine-generated claim status.

## Descriptive framing patterns

Insert construct frequencies and the distribution of overreach severity. Do not generalize beyond the sampled corpus unless all confirmatory gates pass.

## Primary associations

Report coefficients or mean differences with uncertainty intervals and adjusted q-values. Use association language, not causal language.

## Robustness and sensitivity

Report ordinal-model comparison, high-overreach binary specification, leave-one-group-out checks, and missing-quality sensitivity. Explicitly name unavailable checks.

## Limitations

Address source concentration, selection into media coverage, article–market matching uncertainty, temporal market-quality availability, coding provenance, and the inability of observational associations to establish causality.

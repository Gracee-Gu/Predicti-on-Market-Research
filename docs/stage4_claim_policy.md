# Stage 4 Claim and Evidence Policy

The pipeline labels outputs as `confirmatory_ready`, `exploratory_only`, or `blocked`.

`confirmatory_ready` requires every claim gate to pass. `exploratory_only` means calculations are valid but corpus coverage or provenance prevents population-level claims. `blocked` means required columns, valid values, or minimum sample size are absent.

Allowed wording for exploratory outputs: “within this corpus,” “is associated with,” and “descriptively.” Prohibited wording without confirmatory readiness: “media generally,” “causes,” “proves,” “the public,” or platform-wide generalization.

AI-assisted coding must remain identifiable. AI agreement is not equivalent to independent human intercoder reliability. A human-reviewed adjudication field or documented provenance record is required for confirmatory claims.

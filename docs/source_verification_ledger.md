## Rules

- “Verified” means that title, authorship, venue, year, and where applicable DOI were checked against a journal, publisher, institutional repository, or official author page.
- Working papers are never described as peer-reviewed.
- Contemporary journalism will be used to define cases and partnerships, not to establish academic causal claims.
- Before final submission, the bibliography should be compiled and DOI links spot-checked in a clean environment.

# Stage 7 Acceptance Criteria

## Research acceptance

The study is `finalization_ready` only when all of the following are true:

1. the corpus contains at least the configured minimum number of documents;
2. at least three substantively different source types are represented;
3. both platform-owned and independent-media samples exceed the minimum per-group count;
4. human-audited coding evidence exists;
5. reliability statistics are reported for core framing constructs;
6. article–market matching is traceable;
7. publication timestamps are sufficiently available;
8. publication-aligned market-quality evidence covers at least half of analyzed article–market records;
9. raw market-quality measures are retained;
10. market quality is not represented by cumulative volume alone;
11. case-level evidence can trace high-overreach texts to their market records;
12. conclusions preserve the observational and within-corpus limits.

## Delivery acceptance

When research acceptance passes, Stage 7 must produce:

- complete English manuscript;
- appendix;
- tables and figures referenced consistently;
- reproducibility instructions;
- clean-environment execution notes;
- final PDF;
- supervisor-style critical review;
- secret and copyright audit;
- claim-to-evidence audit.

## Recovery acceptance

A repository may pass Stage 7 implementation while failing research acceptance. In that case, the correct output is:

```text
empirical_recovery_required
```

with a complete, executable recovery plan. It must not create a document labeled as a final empirical paper.

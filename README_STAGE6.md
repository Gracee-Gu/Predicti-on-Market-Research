# Stage 6 — Final Submission, Reproducibility Capsule, and Defense Readiness

Stage 6 converts the Stage 5 manuscript and evidence ledger into a final, auditable submission package.

It does **not** strengthen the empirical claim status. The Stage 4/5 `claim_status` remains authoritative.
The workflow:

1. audits the Stage 5 manuscript and evidence package;
2. creates a clean and a blinded manuscript copy;
3. builds a submission metadata sheet and data-availability statement;
4. creates a compact reproducibility capsule;
5. generates a defense brief and supervisor handoff memo;
6. performs a final integrity and claim-boundary audit.

Start with `docs/runbook_stage6.md`.
